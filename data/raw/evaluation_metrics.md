Recall at K measures the fraction of relevant documents retrieved within the top K results. Mean Reciprocal Rank (MRR) computes the reciprocal of the rank of the first relevant document. Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain (NDCG) accounts for the position of relevant items using logarithmic decay weighting. Hit Rate at K is a binary metric returning 1 if any relevant document appears in the top K, and 0 otherwise.
